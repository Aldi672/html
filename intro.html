Introduction

PT. Haekal Maritim Indonesia is a comprehensive maritime services provider, specializing in ship management, ship agency, and a broad spectrum of shipping support services. Our offerings encompass the full range of ship requirements, including but not limited to underwater operations, safety equipment supply, ship repair brokerage, and port clearance facilitation for customers across the globe.
