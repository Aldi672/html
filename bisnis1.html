Business Overview

PT. Haekal Maritim Indonesia is a distinguished provider of comprehensive maritime services, dedicated to ensuring the safety, efficiency, and reliability of maritime operations. Our business is structured around three core areas, each offering a suite of specialized services tailored to the diverse needs of the global shipping industry.

Shipping Line

1. Ship Management: Comprehensive management services to ensure vessels are maintained to the highest standards and comply with all international regulations.

2. Ship Operation: Efficient and effective management of vessel operations, handling all aspects from scheduling and logistics to crew management and maintenance.

3. Ship Brokerage: Professional ship brokerage services assisting clients in buying and selling vessels, leveraging our extensive network and market expertise for optimal terms and support.

Shipping Agency

1. Ship Agency Services: Expert ship agency services ensuring smooth and efficient port operations, handling documentation, logistics, and coordination with port authorities.

2. Port Clearance: Seamless port clearance processes to minimize delays and ensure compliance with regulatory requirements.

Shipping Support

1. Ship Supplies: Provision of necessary provisions and equipment, ensuring vessels are well-stocked and prepared for their voyages.

2. Warehousing: Secure and efficient storage solutions for maritime cargo and equipment.

3. Underwater Work: Specialized underwater services including inspections, maintenance, and repairs, utilizing advanced technology and skilled divers.

4. Safety Equipment Inspection & Repair: Comprehensive repair services for safety equipment, ensuring compliance with international maritime safety standards.
